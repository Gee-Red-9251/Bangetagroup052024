About this website


Papua New Guinea has three major national languages.One is English and the other two are Motu and Pidgin also known as Tok Pisin.The aim of this site is to assist in learning Pidgin language which is the common spoken language through out Papua New Guinea.You may find Tok Pisin as fun but its quite enjoyable once you engage it in a conversation.

About Tok Pisin:

What is Tok Pisin ?

Tok Pisin is a creole language spoken by millions in Papua New Guinea.

Why learn Tok Pisin ?

Understanding Tok Pisin allows you to connect with locals,immerse in the culture,and nevigate daily life in Papua New Guinea more effectively.
